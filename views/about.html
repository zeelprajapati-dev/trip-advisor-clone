About TripAdvisor Business Trip Advisor

TripAdvisor helps busy teams, founders and travel agents turn messy travel plans into clean, book-ready itineraries. We blend human-style planning with AI so every business trip feels thoughtful, efficient and on-budget.

Built for modern business travel
Live + AI planning

TripAdvisor Business Trip Advisor is your always-on trip planner. Share where your team is travelling from, your meeting schedule and budget — we return curated packages with:

  • Optimised flights, stays and local transport.
  • Day-wise agendas with buffers for traffic and meetings.
  • Suggested restaurants and work-friendly cafés.
  • Optional add-ons like airport transfers and local experiences.

Everything is designed so you can move from idea to book-ready itinerary in minutes, not weeks of back-and-forth.

Who is it for?
From solo founders to large teams
  • Travel agents who need a fast way to design repeatable, high-quality packages for clients.
  • Sales & partnership teams hopping between cities for demos, pitches and roadshows.
  • Founders & CXOs who want a single view of where their team is staying and what it costs.
  • HR & admin teams tasked with keeping travel budgets predictable and compliant.

Use TripAdvisor as your planning copilot — you stay in control, we handle the structure, maths and logistics.

How Business TripAdvisor works

1. Start from the dashboard and share your origin cities, destination and rough dates. 2. Use AI Trips to co-design a day-wise plan with our TripAdvisor AI. 3. Pick or tweak a package, then confirm using the booking flow you already know.

Every package you book becomes part of your private library, so agents and admins can re-use what works and improve it over time.
